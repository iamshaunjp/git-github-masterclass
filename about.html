About 8.BIT.BLOG

Welcome to 8.BIT.BLOG, your ultimate destination for everything retro gaming! Founded in 2020 by passionate gaming enthusiasts, we've dedicated ourselves to celebrating the rich history of video games while exploring how classic gaming continues to influence modern game design.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding gaming's past is essential to appreciating its future. Our mission is to bridge the gap between nostalgic gaming memories and contemporary gaming culture, providing insights, reviews, and tutorials that matter to both veteran gamers and newcomers alike.
